Our History


Started in 2017, Bramble's Squirrel Service has been slowly but surely decreasing the population of squirrels worldwide. Throughout the last five years, we have made leaps and bounds in squirrel-catching technology including that have completely changed the game. Since we first began, we are now able to catch squirrels more efficiently than ever before!

Our Mission


  • We aim to cut all squirrels in half by 2026
  • We strive for a squirrel-free society where trees are free to not have squirrels in them
  • We firmly believe that squirrels are the cause of all evil and strife in the world and therefore must be eradicated once and for all
